ABOUT US

A company that serves as a hub for high-quality seeds. We are producer and exporter of agricultural commodities, emphasizing high-quality products sourced from farmers in Thailand to fully satisfy the needs of consumers.

OUR PRIME MOMENTS

Royal Thai Seeds was founded in 2011 by an engineer with a passion for agriculture. We developed a machine to separate and clean basil seeds to control their quality, ensuring they are as uniform in size as the customer desires. The seeds must be clean and pure, with as few spoiled or atrophied seeds as possible. Our aim is to produce aflatoxin-free food.

We have collaborated with the Chulalongkorn University Institute of Biotechnology and Genetic Engineering to research and develop the production of basil seeds free from aflatoxin contamination.
